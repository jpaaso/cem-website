Join our lively and dynamic Customer Experience Management team whose goal is to revolutionise the face of digital customer experiences.

You have a deep understanding of turning technology into business benefits and a strong track record of delivering diverse digital projects. You’re able to spot the emerging global trends and capitalise on them. You are a passionate, structured, self-driven and committed team player with excellent communication and networking skills.

We are seeking smart, entrepreneurial and tech-savvy consultants’ with experience in the retail or finance sectors. You have a deep understanding of the customers’ business needs coupled with technology insight and the confidence to challenge their current working models. Side by side with our innovative sales, service and solution teams you will offer new visions of future customer experiences.

We offer consultancy positions in Finland and Sweden are for candidates with one or more of the following skills up their sleeve:

  • Tackling the challenges of the digital transformation and changing business landscape
  • Background in mobility, HTML5 or Middlewaremiddleware
  • Experience in eCommerce assingments assignments such as visioning, strategy definition, business case creation, road mapping, service design and requirement gathering
  • Experience in Microsoft Business Intelligence
  • Advanced analytics (tools like SPSS, SAS and R).
  • Web analytics (tools like Google Analytics, IBM Digital Analytics, Adobe Analytics).
