Solution Head

Are you a natural-born leader able to shine a light on new solutions in the digital services arena? Tieto’s Customer Experience Management team is seeking visionary Solution Heads to play a pivotal role in developing our product and solution offering in the Nordic region.

As part of your entrepreneurial mindset, you have masterful networking skills combined with a razor-sharp understanding of the major business challenges facing digital channels. Furthermore, you have the vision to decipher a concrete roadmap for delivering solutions for these challenges.

As a tech-savvy and eager follower of the latest digital trends, you have the insight to identify the most suitable platforms to match our customers’ needs and the confidence to try out new ones.

You identify the possibilities in such areas as

  • E-Commerce
  • Web experience / digital channels
  • Customer intelligence or
  • Social and business productivity, which sets to improve our customers’ collaboration and information flow with employees and partners through social platforms enabled by the cloud.

You have a background preferably in retail or financial services and a solid history of innovating and developing commerce or banking business applications. The ideal candidate is persuasive, passionate and influential – a thought leader with the ability to inspire others to achieve great results. If you are the visionary commander we are after and have got what it takes to lead our talented team of professionals, we are eagerly waiting to hear from you. Help take Tieto’s customer experience offering to the next level.
