Visionary

Service and UX Designers

We are searching for top design talent to fill junior and senior positions in Finland and in Sweden. Whether you are a seasoned designer hungry for challenge or in the early years of your design career, come and flex your creative muscles at Tieto and share your vision with us.

We are looking for passionate individuals to breathe life into revolutionary new service experiences. Seeing a new service idea or design after another ‘get lost in translation’ is not enough for you. You want your concepts and designs to have an impact, resonate with the everyday lives of thousands of people and see them become reality.

To help mastermind the next generation of customer experiences, you have the flexibility and talent to deliver top-notch design work to tight deadlines. You fluently combine human, business and technology viewpoints, and exercise customer listening. You master the standard designer’s software repertoire and have designing experience for multiple platforms. You may be a wizard in prototyping or a creative business-model genius. Most of all you enjoy working closely with customers to solve complex business and design problems and lead the race in driving new visionary service experiences.

Regardless of your background in design, business, technology or psychology, you must challenge us and our clients with your creative skills and design thinking. Join our unique design crew to get your hands on the most interesting and game-changing work in the world.

This opportunity allows great flexibility in your work and gives you an unparalleled chance to grow as part of a passionate and fun design team.
