Project Manager

Do you have a history of delivering successful digital solutions and a passion for customer satisfaction? We have an exciting opportunity for a natural-born leader who can fuse impressive knowledge of the digital industry with creative ideas.

The ideal candidate has extensive practical project management experience from leading fully agile projects as well. You have a good grasp of the technical nitty-gritty involved in digital projects, and creating team spirit comes naturally to you.

You will be a key player in the day-to-day planning and managing of projects, which requires impeccable organisational skills and the confidence to claim ownership. You will be the rock that remains calm under pressure, ensuring your team is constantly motivated in their pursuit of world-class user experiences.

You have a healthy understanding of demanding architectures, solutions and services ideally in the field of online services or Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing. Most importantly, you need to have the passion and commitment for aspiring for the highest standards, while always placing the client at the heart of everything we do.

If you are experienced in leading global multicultural projects and services, have knowledge of the financial services industry or retail processes, and want to lead a bunch of highly talented professionals, Tieto could be your place.

Join our ambitious journey to shape the future of digital experiences. We are looking for candidates in both Finland and Sweden.
