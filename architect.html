Architect

Do you dream about creating cutting edge customer experiences to keep up with today’s technology development? We are on the lookout for leading architects to spearhead Tieto’s Customer Experience Management offering.

As a solutions architect your role is to understand our customer’s business and guide them towards integrating the latest technology into their services. You will lead the technical design of services and act as a fundamental link between our clients and Tieto’s development teams. We also expect you to bring insight into sales support.

You are passionate and curious about new technologies and have a background as an architect or technical lead. You have the confidence and ability to inspire clients by communicating a compelling technical vision, and creating value through strategic and technological dimensions of an opportunity.

In order to join our Customer Experience Management offering team, we wish you to have expertise in one or more of the following areas:

  • Seniority with Java applications, J2EE, Javascript, AngluarJS, Rest.li, or Microsoft, especially regarding digital channel solutions for financial services or retail
  • Drupal, EPiServer, WordPress or other modern CMS technologies
  • Latest front-end and mobile technologies including full stack understanding (front-end, middleware, backend, such as HTML5, Android, iOS, Windows Phone, CSS; Spring, Java2EE, PhoneGap, Cordova, DataPower, IBM Worklight)
  • Challenging BI/DW architectures and implementations, preferably experience in utilising Big Data in bringing business value and customer insight.
  • Master Data Management processes and technologies with several years of experience in creating MDM architectures and key responsibilities in MDM implementations.


We are seeking business-savvy and technically brilliant architects to join our winning teams in both Finland and Sweden.
