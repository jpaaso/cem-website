Join our lively and dynamic Customer Experience Management team whose goal is to revolutionise the face of digital customer experiences

If you have a record of delivering diverse digital projects and can spot and capitalise on emerging global trends about to take off, you could be exactly what we are looking for. You are a passionate, structured, self-driven and committed team player with excellent communication and networking skills.

We are seeking smart, entrepreneurial consultants with a strong experience in the retail or finance sectors to expand Tieto’s business in Finland and Sweden. You have a deep understanding of the customers’ business needs and the confidence to challenge their current working models. Side by side with our innovative sales, service and solution teams, you will offer new visions of future customer experiences that will excite and inspire.

Consultancy positions are on offer for those who have one or more of the following skills up their sleeves:

  • Tackling challenges of the digital transformation and changing business landscape
  • Experience in assignments such as visioning, strategy definition, business case creation, road mapping, service design and requirement gathering
  • Deep industry knowledge within retail and finance
  • Ability to create trust and deliver to C-level

Got what it takes to be a leading figure in this exciting new sector?
